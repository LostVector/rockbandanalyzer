Welcome to the Rock Band Analyzer

Posted by rkuo on May 4, 2009  •  Comments (0)  •  Full article

The Rock Band Analyzer is a special site that I designed to parse, generate, and manage all kinds of interesting data about Rock Band songs. For example, find out the highest scoring paths through a song on a particular instrument, the distribution of songs released so far by genre, or how many songs have been released by a particular artist.

This site is under very early development, but the foundation is there for some really cool features to be released in the future. Stay tuned!
